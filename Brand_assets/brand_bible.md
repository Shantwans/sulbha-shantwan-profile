# SULBHA SHANTWAN
# BRAND BIBLE

Version 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

This document defines the visual, emotional, verbal, and experiential identity of Sulbha Shantwan.

Every design decision, page layout, interaction, illustration, animation, photograph, article, and user experience must align with this brand.

The goal is not to build a website.

The goal is to build an unforgettable learning universe.

---

# BRAND ESSENCE

## WHO IS SULBHA?

Sulbha is:

A Learning Experience Designer.

A storyteller.

A creator.

A researcher.

A homeschooler.

An innovator.

A builder of educational ecosystems.

A guide helping people navigate learning, creativity, technology and human development.

---

# BRAND PROMISE

Visitors should leave feeling:

Thoughtful.

Hopeful.
