# SULBHA WEBSITE CONTENT STRATEGY

## PURPOSE

This document serves as the content blueprint for Sulbha Shantwan's personal website.

The website is not a portfolio.

It is a digital ecosystem that showcases:

- Learning Design
- Educational Innovation
- Thought Leadership
- Storytelling
- Creativity
- Research
- Entrepreneurship
- Personal Exploration

The goal is to create a website visitors return to repeatedly.

---

# WEBSITE GOALS

The website should:

1. Establish credibility
2. Demonstrate expertise
3. Showcase creativity
4. Highlight innovation
5. Attract collaborations
6. Generate speaking opportunities
7. Support Rooted Labs
8. Build a community
9. Archive ideas and projects
10. Create intellectual legacy
